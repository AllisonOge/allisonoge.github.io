I’m Ogechukwu Allison Kanu, an MSc researcher focused on machine learning for wireless and signal data. I enjoy building practical ML pipelines—mostly PyTorch—plus the docs and experiments that make them repeatable. My current groove: self-supervised pretraining, multi-task evaluation, and small, steady improvements that add up. Outside the lab, you’ll find me studying French, exploring new cafés, and collecting ideas for projects I can ship in a weekend.
